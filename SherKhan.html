Sher Khan

I am Sher Khan, a tiger who is the main antagonist in the story.

Friends: Shere Khan was best friends with Baloo, Bagheera, Louie, Hathi, and Kaa.
However, he was egotistical and cocky which sometimes led to situations where his friends had to bail him out.

Enemies: Baloo, Bagheera and King Louie.
